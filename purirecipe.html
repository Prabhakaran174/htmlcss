puri

Poori or Puri is a traditional Indian fried bread that is delicious to enjoy with almost any main dish. It is a simple unleavened bread made from just whole wheat flour, salt, and water.Small balls of dough are rolled and flattened, and quickly fried in hot oil. The poori puffs up to create light, crispy pillows that just beg to be bitten into. Inside is soft and tender, and perfect for using to scoop up a hearty curry.

prep time

35 min.

cook time

25 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : beef breakfast pancakes food

instructions

step 1

take the whole wheat flour, salt and oil or melted ghee.

step 2

Divide the dough into small or medium pieces about 25 to 30

step 3

Heat oil in a deep frying pan or kadai

ingredients

3 cups whole wheat flour

1 teaspoon oil

1 teaspoon salt
